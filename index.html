About the opportunity

Coherence Engine™ is a patent-pending systems architecture being developed to detect coherence, drift, instability, recovery, and hidden breakdown across complex systems.

The existing foundation includes research, technical concepts, application pathways, pilot materials, and commercialization opportunities across AI, enterprise systems, infrastructure, energy, health and wellness, and other data-rich environments.

This role is for an experienced operator prepared to help establish capital, infrastructure, partnerships, governance, and near-term commercial traction.

What you will help lead

  • Establish a practical company operating plan and near-term financial runway
  • Restore essential websites, professional email, hosting, and business infrastructure
  • Prioritize the strongest initial commercial pathway
  • Package the first paid diagnostic, pilot, licensing opportunity, or enterprise engagement
  • Develop fundraising, revenue, partnership, and licensing strategies
  • Help recruit specialized leadership for portfolio ventures
  • Create operating systems, financial controls, and milestone accountability
  • Help secure customers, strategic partners, investors, or licensees

Strong candidate profile

  • Startup leadership as a founder, CEO, COO, venture builder, or operating partner
  • Experience commercializing technical research or intellectual property
  • Enterprise software, analytics, infrastructure, or technology-platform experience
  • Fundraising, licensing, strategic partnership, or investor-relations capability
  • Direct investment capacity or verifiable access to committed capital
  • A record of converting early-stage concepts into revenue-generating businesses
  • The judgment to focus a broad opportunity into a credible first market

Capital and operating expectations

The selected partner must be capable of helping establish near-term runway through direct company investment, verifiable investor access, paid customer relationships, financed operating resources, revenue-producing partnerships, or hands-on executive execution.

Important: A promise to raise money at an unspecified future date will not, by itself, qualify someone for co-founder equity.

Compensation and equity

This is currently an equity-based co-founder opportunity, not a salaried position. Founder compensation may be established after sufficient funding or revenue has been secured.

  • Structured evaluation period
  • Defined responsibilities and milestones
  • Vesting over time
  • Continued active contribution
  • Formal confidentiality and intellectual property agreements
  • Board and founder approval where applicable

Intellectual property boundary

The existing Coherence Engine™ framework, patent filings, research, operators, terminology, documentation, technical architecture, and other pre-existing materials will remain protected as founder-controlled background intellectual property.

This role does not automatically include ownership in Cohera.AI, RecoverMargins™, RootWell & Star™, or other portfolio ventures. Any cross-venture rights must be separately documented and approved.
